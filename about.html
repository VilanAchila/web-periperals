Welcome

EWIS Peripherals (Pvt) Ltd., formally a division of East-West Information Systems Ltd since 1986, and then E-W Information systems Ltd, was incorporated as a separate company on the 7th of July 1999. From it's humble beginning as a division of East-West Information Systems Ltd. to being a company of its own, has grown from a traditional device and consumable sales organization to a solution-based organization. The noteworthy partnership of 29 years with Lexmark International, a global print giant, since 1991, has contributed towards the company’s success of developing the Lexmark brand in the country to be one of the top Printer brands in the consumer minds.

Moving from the traditional printer sales, the company had ventured into Manage Printer Services with intelligent devices of Lexmark products to gain the highest market share of Manage Print Services in the country. The company is also the Distributor for Riello Power products, an Italian brand of Uninterrupted Power Supply products for the past 15 years with significant installations of units in the UPS market.

Expanding on the solutions to the customers the company partners with Konica Minolta group for Copiers products and Production printing equipment since 2013 provide a holistic solution to our customers who require printing in other areas other than laser printers. The addition of Casio projectors, Kodak scanning equipment and OCTA Matrix band of dot-matrix printers along with EWIS Solar power solution complement the product portfolio of the company to provide a total solution to our valued customers over the past few years. The dynamic, young management team along with a staff strength of just below 100, is geared to provide customer-centric and value-added services to our customers while promoting our partners' products and solutions to the market. With an annual revenue of LKR 1.3 Million, (US$ 7 Million) the company has grown to be a significant contributor to the EWIS group.

The company was recognized as one of the top 20 company in Sri Lanka by “Great Place to Work” Sri Lanka consecutively for the past 3 years. This means the employees Trust the people they work for, have Pride in what they do and enjoy their work. As a group and company that recognizes not only business but corporate social responsibility as an important and significant factor in today’s business environment, EWIS Peripherals is pleased to state that the company’s initiative towards a residential homes for less fortunate children in the society, and as an environmental initiative investment into developing land to increase the carbon footprint, and the green initiative of collection of toner cartridges is just to name a few.
